PAN Card Verification

As per RBI guidelines, PAN verification is mandatory for all banking transactions. Please provide your PAN details exactly as they appear on your PAN card.

Format: 5 letters, 4 digits, 1 letter (e.g., ABCDE1234F)
Important: Providing incorrect PAN details may lead to transaction failures and penalties as per Income Tax regulations.
Why PAN Verification?
PAN verification is mandatory for all financial transactions above ₹50,000 as per Income Tax Department rules.
Benefits of PAN Linking
• Faster income tax returns processing
• Easy tracking of financial transactions
• Required for high-value transactions
Don't Have a PAN?
You can apply for a new PAN card online through NSDL or UTIITSL portals.
